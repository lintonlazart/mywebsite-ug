Cody Gakpo - Liverpool Forward Updates: A Mid-Season Assessment (December 2025)

Did you know that just two seasons ago, many pundits debated Cody Gakpo's optimal position at Liverpool, questioning if he truly possessed the prolificacy required of a central forward in the Premier League? As of December 2025, those discussions have largely subsided, replaced by widespread recognition of his crucial, evolving role within Jürgen Klopp's (or his successor's, assuming continuity in strategy) dynamic attacking system.

The Dutch international has blossomed into one of Liverpool's most consistent and versatile attackers, with his performances directly influencing the club's fortunes in both domestic and European competitions. This article dives deep into the latest performances of Cody Gakpo, offering comprehensive updates, expert analysis, and what his continued development means for Liverpool and the broader landscape of UK football.

We will analyse Gakpo's latest performances, his goal-scoring contributions, and his tactical significance, providing a crucial mid-season snapshot for fans and football enthusiasts alike. Expect real statistics, insights from credible UK sources, and a look ahead at what the future holds for this formidable Liverpool forward.

The Current Situation: Gakpo's Ascendancy in December 2025

As the Premier League season approaches its festive period in December 2025, Cody Gakpo stands as a testament to patience, adaptation, and consistent improvement. His journey from an initial period of tactical experimentation to becoming an indispensable part of Liverpool's attacking trident has been remarkable. Recent developments highlight his growing influence across all competitions.

In the 2025/26 season so far, Gakpo has featured in 22 matches across the Premier League, Champions League, and Carabao Cup. He has directly contributed to 14 goals, comprising 8 goals and 6 assists. These figures represent a significant uptick in consistency compared to his earlier seasons, where flashes of brilliance were sometimes interspersed with quieter periods.

"Cody Gakpo's evolution into a truly complete forward has been a joy to watch. His ability to link play, press relentlessly, and now consistently find the back of the net makes him invaluable to Liverpool's title aspirations this season," observed Jamie Carragher on Sky Sports' Monday Night Football last week.

His impact is particularly felt in crucial fixtures. In the recent 3-1 victory over Manchester United at Old Trafford, Gakpo netted the opening goal and provided a key assist, earning him Man of the Match honours. This kind of decisive contribution in high-stakes games underscores his growing maturity and importance.

The statistical narrative around Gakpo also points to an increased efficiency. His shot conversion rate has climbed to 21% this season, up from 15% in the previous campaign, indicating improved composure and finishing. Furthermore, his progressive carries per 90 minutes have risen, demonstrating his enhanced ability to drive play forward from deeper positions, a vital attribute in Liverpool's system.

Recent Developments and Tactical Nuances

A key development in Gakpo's updates is his increasing proficiency in a hybrid role, often drifting between a nominal left-wing position and a central false nine. This tactical flexibility allows Liverpool to fluidly switch formations mid-game, creating headaches for opposition defences. He is equally adept at holding up play as he is at making intelligent runs in behind.

According to data from Opta Analysts, Gakpo's expected goals (xG) per 90 minutes currently stands at 0.55, placing him among the top forwards in the league. This metric suggests that he is consistently getting into high-quality scoring positions, a testament to his movement and understanding of Liverpool's attacking patterns. His partnership with the likes of Mohamed Salah and Darwin Núñez has become increasingly telepathic, creating a formidable front line.

His work rate off the ball remains a hallmark of his game, leading the line in Liverpool's famed counter-pressing system. This season, he ranks highly among Premier League forwards for successful pressures in the attacking third, a statistic that underlines his defensive contribution and willingness to win the ball back high up the pitch.

Background & Context: Gakpo's Journey to Anfield Stardom

Cody Gakpo's arrival at Liverpool in January 2023 was met with considerable excitement, yet also a degree of scrutiny. Coming from PSV Eindhoven, where he had been a prolific winger and captain, the transition to the Premier League and adapting to Klopp's demanding system was always going to be a challenge. In the Eredivisie, Gakpo had accumulated an impressive 55 goals and 50 assists in 159 appearances, a record that clearly demonstrated his attacking pedigree.

His initial months at Anfield saw him primarily deployed as a central forward, a role he had played sparingly at PSV. This period was crucial for his adaptation, allowing him to acclimatise to the pace and physicality of English football. While his goal tally wasn't immediately prolific, his intelligent link-up play, work rate, and ability to create chances for others were evident.

Important milestones include his first Premier League goal against Everton in February 2023, a significant moment that kick-started his confidence. The subsequent 2023/24 season saw him cement his place, particularly towards the latter half, as he began to find a more consistent rhythm. He finished that season with 12 goals across all competitions, a solid return that showcased his progress.

Compared to past situations of foreign forwards adapting to the Premier League, Gakpo's journey has been relatively smooth, albeit with its learning curves. Players like Roberto Firmino, whom Gakpo has often been compared to in terms of his 'false nine' capabilities, also took time to fully integrate and refine their game under Klopp. Gakpo's trajectory mirrors this pattern of gradual refinement leading to sustained excellence.

The trust placed in him by the coaching staff has been paramount. They recognised his raw talent and potential, providing the necessary support and tactical guidance to help him evolve. This long-term vision has now paid dividends, transforming him into the dynamic attacking threat we see in December 2025.

Impact & Implications: Gakpo's Influence on Liverpool and UK Football

The sustained excellence of Cody Gakpo has profound implications for Liverpool, influencing their tactical approach, league standing, and even their transfer market strategy. For UK residents, particularly Liverpool fans, his performances are a source of immense pride and optimism, directly impacting the club's pursuit of silverware.

His ability to perform consistently at a high level provides Liverpool with crucial attacking depth and versatility. This means less reliance on any single player, making the team more robust against injuries or dips in form. When Gakpo delivers, it significantly boosts Liverpool's chances of winning, directly affecting the mood and morale of millions of supporters across Merseyside and beyond.

From a tactical perspective, Gakpo's hybrid role allows Liverpool to be unpredictable. He can drop deep to facilitate midfield runners, stretch defences with his pace, or occupy centre-backs, creating space for wide players like Salah. This tactical flexibility is a nightmare for opposition managers, who struggle to contain Liverpool's fluid attack.

"Gakpo's intelligence on the pitch is often overlooked. He understands space and movement better than most forwards in the league, which is why he's so vital to how Liverpool play, whether they're breaking down a low block or hitting on the counter," stated a recent analysis in The Guardian.

Economically, a thriving Gakpo enhances Liverpool's brand value and commercial appeal. High-performing players attract more global attention, increase shirt sales, and potentially lead to more lucrative sponsorship deals. For the broader Premier League, his success adds to the narrative of high-quality talent, making the league even more attractive to international audiences.

While the impact of a star player like Gakpo is felt nationally, there are distinct regional implications. Expert Insights & Analysis: What the Pundits are Saying

The footballing world in December 2025 is largely in agreement: Cody Gakpo has firmly established himself as a top-tier Premier League forward.
